About Us

At ALDOJ HOMES AND PROPERTIES, we understand that buying a home is one of the biggest investments you will make in your lifetime. That's why we are dedicated to providing our clients with a seamless and stress-free experience when it comes to finding their dream home.

As a subsidiary of ALDOJ AMAZING CONSULT, we have a wealth of expertise and experience in helping our clients achieve their goals in human capacity development, financial management and agricultural investments. With our introduction of THE TEAM HOUSING PROJECT, we aim to make your housing dreams a reality.

Built on the principle of teamwork, the TEAM HOUSING PROJECT is a collaborative effort between four individuals, each with the goal of building a beautiful 3-bedroom bungalow in three years. At the end of the project, each subscriber will be the proud owner of their own new home.

With each bungalow priced at forty million naira (40,000,000), we understand that buying a home can be an overwhelming financial commitment. That's why we have structured the TEAM HOUSING PROJECT in a way that allows subscribers to make manageable monthly payments of N1,200,000 for the duration of three years. In the event that a subscriber is unable to complete the project within three years, the project will attract a 10% increase (annually) of the total sum to the defaulted subscriber.

Located in a serene and tranquil estate, each bungalow sits on a 300 square meter flat land, surrounded by greenery and a sense of class. Each home boasts a spacious living room, a cozy porch, a driveway that can accommodate up to three cars, and a green area for outdoor activities. With a Certificate of Occupancy (C of O) for the land, you can rest assured that you are making a solid investment in your future.
